Why Trees so important?

TREES CLEAN THE AIR

Trees absorb odors and pollutant gases and filter particulates our of the air by trapping them on their leaves and bark.

TREES PROVIDES OXYGEN

In one year an acre of mature trees can provide enough oxygen for 18 people. Peepal tree is most popular trees that emit oxygen in the air.

TREES SAVE WATER

Most newly planted trees need only fiften gallons of water a week. As trees transpire, they increases atmospheric moisture.

TREES PROVIDES FOOD

An apple tree can yield up to 15-20 bushels of fruit per year and can be planted on the tiniest urban lot.
